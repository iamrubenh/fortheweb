About

Small Beginnings

This wonderful business idea started off with me, Ruben, thinking of a way i could supply Belizeans with quality hardware. I often overheard locals expressing their desire for a company that supplies durable international recognised quality hardware. So i came up with the idea to supply homes with these types of hardware. They are wonderful sinks and faucets that will have your home looking great for a long time.


Beautiful hardware for all our beautiful customers.
