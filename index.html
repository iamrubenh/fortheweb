Expertise

What to expect from me.

Email marketing is one of the best tools you can use to leverage more traction either to drive sales or just to simply promote your business. Almost every growing business that I know of, use email to communicate important events or promotions to their subscribed customers. The use of email marketing is an effective way for you to sell your products.

You want your customers to have the best experience navigating your copy in their emails and because of that, the UI is very important. I constantly keep up with trends to see what other developers are doing and how I can improve my campaigns. Responsiveness, Loading time, geometry and UI are just a few of the things I keep a keen eye for. I always do my best to make sure that all content is presented in a very moderate and proportionate way, making use of traditional design techniques.
